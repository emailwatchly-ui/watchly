PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account (email address), submitting crime reports (location, incident details, category), and using the app (device information, usage patterns).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to display crime reports on the community map, improve the app experience, moderate submitted content, and communicate important updates. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Location Data

Location is only accessed when you submit a report or use the map. You may enable location masking to offset your reported pin by approximately 75 metres for privacy. We do not track your location continuously or in the background.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure hosted in Sydney, Australia. We implement industry-standard security measures including row-level security and encrypted connections.

5. Report Moderation

All submitted reports are reviewed by moderators before appearing publicly on the map. Moderators may reject reports that are inaccurate, inappropriate, or violate our community guidelines.

6. Push Notifications

If you grant permission, we may send push notifications about crime activity in your area. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings. Your push token is stored securely and never shared with third parties.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at emailwatchly@gmail.com. You may also view your own reports within the app at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Watchly is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at emailwatchly@gmail.com.
